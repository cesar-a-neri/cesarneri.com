Mexico 44


B.Arch Thesis investigating issues of sociocultural identity in the indigenous communities of the Highlands of Chiapas under pressures of Western capitalist modernity. Through the execution of a national rebranding campaign, Mexico 44 gives a critical speculation on the value of these subverted cultures under the current globalized landscape.
