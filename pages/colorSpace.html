Color Space


Colorspace is an interactive sculpture that translates text messages into  animations of colored light. As people enter the space, they pass a long marble wall of suspended light pendants. Anybody can send a text message to the number printed on the wall. The sculpture replies with a sparkling wash of light that sweeps across the wall, illuminating the space with new colors.
